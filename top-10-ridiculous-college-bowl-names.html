Top 10 Ridiculous College Bowl Names

Publish date: 2024-06-12

When a game is named after a little-known family pub chain with outlets in only 23 states (and one too many apostrophes), perhaps it’s a sign that the college bowl system has hit rock bottom? On Dec. 21 Louisvillle beat Southern Mississippi, 31-28, in the Beef ‘O’ Brady’s Bowl, played in St. Petersburg, Fla. The game’s announced attendance, 20,017, was the lowest for any bowl game in five years.
