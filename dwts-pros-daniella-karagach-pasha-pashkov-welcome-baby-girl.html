Dancing with the Stars pro dancers Daniella Karagach and Pasha Pashkov have officially welcomed their first child together. Karagach gave birth to their baby daughter on Tuesday, and the couple has already shared her name with fans.

Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov Welcome First Baby

Karagach and Pashkov announced the news of their daughter’s birth on Instagram, sharing a few photos from the hospital as they held the newborn, who was swaddled in a pink cloth with a matching headband. They shared that the baby girl’s name is Nikita.

The couple announced that they were expecting a baby last November, and they later shared that they would be having a daughter. Earlier this year, the couple embarked on a tropical babymoon together ahead of Nikita’s birth.

The couple’s DWTS co-stars and friends shared their excitement about the birth on Instagram. Fellow pro Witney Carson wrote, “She’s so perfect omg!” Maksim Chmerkovskiy commented, “How many times can I like it?! Congratulations you two!!!”

Karagach also posted a brief clip of Nikita on Instagram stories, calling her a “daddy’s girl.” She also pointed out that both Nikita and Pashkov are Geminis.

Several DWTS Pros Have Been Expecting Babies

It seems to be baby season for Dancing with the Stars pros. In January, Jenna Johnson and Val Chmerkovskiy welcomed a baby boy named Rome. Then in May, Lindsay Arnold and Witney Carson both gave birth to their second children.

Meanwhile, Peta Murgatroyd and Maksim Chmerkovskiy are currently expecting their second child together, due very soon. The couple shared back in April that they’re expecting another boy, with their son Shai preparing to become a big brother.

Two of the newborn DWTS babies have already met each other. Last month, Arnold’s daughter June met Carson’s son Jet, as seen in a cute photo posted to Instagram. The two babies were born less than two weeks apart.
