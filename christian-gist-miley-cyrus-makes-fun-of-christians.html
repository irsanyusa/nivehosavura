Miley Cyrus makes fun of Christians

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Referring to herself as a non-judgemental person, in her interview with Paper Magazine, she made fun of Christians who believe the story of Noah’s Ark, saying "That's f--king insane. We've outgrown that fairy tale, like we've outgrown f--king Santa and the tooth fairy."

Adding that "I am literally open to every single thing that is consenting and doesn't involve an animal and everyone is of age. Everything that's legal, I'm down with. Yo, I'm down with any adult, anyone over the age of 18 who is down to love me. I don't relate to being boy or girl, and I don't have to have my partner relate to boy or girl."
