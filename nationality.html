List of Florentine People

  • 1406-01-13 Matteo Palmieri, Florentine humanist and historian (Della vita civile / On Civic Life), born in Florence (d. 1475)
  • 1445-03-01 Sandro Botticelli, Florentine Renaissance painter (Birth of Venus), (d. 1510) probable date of birth
  • 1449-01-01 Lorenzo de' Medici [The Magnificent], Italian Lord of Florence (1469-92), statesman and patron of the arts during the Florentine Renaissance, born in Florence, Republic of Florence (d. 1492)
  • 1472-03-28 Fra Bartolommeo, Florentine Renaissance painter (The Last Judgement), born in Florence (d. 1517)
  • 1502-07-27 Francesco Corteccia, Italian composer, born in Florence, Florentine Republic (d. 1571)
  • 1503-11-17 Il Bronzino, Italian Florentine painter in Mannerist style (Eleanor de Toledo & her Son), born in Florence (d. 1572)
  • 1914-11-14 Florentine Rost van Tonningen (née Heubel), Dutch Nazi supporter known as "Black Widow", born in Amsterdam, Netherlands (d. 2007)
  • 1933-04-28 Karl Heinz Wahren, German pianist and composer (Nocturnal Dances of the Tuscan Virgins in the Florentine Gardens at the Height of the Inquisition), born in Bonn, Germany (d. 2021)
