Winn-Dixie is closing almost 100 stores as it files for bankruptcy see if your store is on the l

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Almost 100 Winn-Dixie, Harveys, and Bi-Lo locations are closing in the Southeast as the regional grocery chains' parent company files for bankruptcy.

On Tuesday, the parent company, Southeastern Grocers, announced it had filed for Chapter 11 bankruptcy protection. The company announced earlier in March it would "voluntarily implement a court-supervised, prepackaged restructuring agreement."

As part of the restructuring, the company is closing 94 grocery stores. That represents about 16% of Southeastern Grocers' store count, with 582 locations remaining open.
