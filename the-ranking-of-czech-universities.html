The Ranking of Czech Universities - Live &amp; Study

Publish date: 2024-06-23

15. 1. 2024 | Study in the Czech Republic

The Czech Republic is a great place for students. According to QS University Ranking topuniversities.com, Czechia has very good results. At Czech universities, you can choose from many fields of study, you are provided with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, get an individual approach, and meet new people. Let’s have a look at some figure-based results for the quality of university education in Czechia.

The best university in the Eastern Europe region

Charles University is ranked as the best university in the Eastern Europe region in 2024. In the top ten of this location-based category, there are five Czech universities. Apart from Charles University, there are also Masaryk University in Brno, the Czech Technical University in Prague, Brno University of Technology, and Palacký University Olomouc.

Best student cities

For 2024, Prague is in the 39th rank when it comes to the best cities for students in the world. When you think about it, Prague is a perfect place for students. There are a lot of places to visit and see, a lot of nice cafés and pleasant pubs to meet with friends, it has great a public transportation system, which gets you anywhere you need. Prague is also a cultural melting pot. You can meet a lot of interesting people from different countries. Being in the heart of Europe, Prague is a convenient starting point for people who want to travel around. Keep in mind that Prague is just one of the location options where you can study in Czechia. There are also other places like Brno, Olomouc, Pilsen, or Pardubice. These are just examples. There are many other great choices you can make.

Ranking by subjects

What about concrete subjects? Let’s have a look at some of them. For Computer Science and Information Systems, the best-ranking Czech university (2023) is the Czech Technical University in Prague. For Agriculture and Forestry, the best-ranking Czech university (2023) is the Czech University of Life Sciences in Prague. For Economics and Econometrics, the best-ranking Czech university (2023) is Charles University. For Art and Design, the best-ranking Czech university (2023) is the Academy of Arts, Architecture and Design Prague.

Ranking can be an interesting way to find out more information about the university you’re interested in. There are many factors to consider. Although you get a better picture of your future alma mater, it’s good to find information from other sources as well. Keep in mind that one school with the best possible ranking might not be the perfect match for everyone. There are so many things that play an important role over here. On the other hand, a university that is less known can provide you with a wonderful education, useful preparation for your future career, and a friendly environment at the same time. Some universities also open new programs. This is something to look into.

To learn about your future school as much as possible, ask students who study there. Also, look at the websites of the schools to see what activities they have to offer. This will help you a lot. It’s worth doing your research. If you are looking for more options, the University Search might be of great help.

Good luck finding the best university option for you!
