This was a busy weekend for everyone’s favorite celebrity couple. Over the weekend, Jennifer Lopez celebrated her 52 birthday, had a yacht day, and confirmed that she is back with Ben Affleck. They were even seen recreating their steamy “Jenny From The Block” yacht PDA.

It’s been rumored for a while, but it’s true, Bennifer is officially back! J-Lo made sure her fans knew that the two were back together when she made it Instagram official on Saturday. Lopez posted super sexy bikini pics in a gallery, and left a surprise for her fans at the end, confirming that she and Affleck are back together. But that is NOT the end of it.

Lopez and Affleck Keep up the PDA

Let’s just say the couple didn’t hold back in St. Tropez this weekend after announcing their reunion. They were seen recreating their steamy yacht scene from the “Jenny From The Block” 2009 music video. They even posed for a camera while getting steamy on the yacht. J-Lo and Affleck were all over each other in these paparazzi shots.

We’re sure you remember the “Jenny From The Block” music video, but if you don’t here’s a refresh. The music video stars both Lopez and Affleck in a super romantic role. They’re seen kissing, riding in cars together, and getting frisky within the video.

Videos of Lopez were posted during what looks to be her birthday dinner. We quickly noticed that in these videos, Affleck and Lopez are seen dancing to “Jenny From The Block.” Coincidence? We don’t think so.

Lopez’s New California Home

The last time the celebrity power couple was seen together before they made it IG official was when they were searching for Lopez’s California mansion.
