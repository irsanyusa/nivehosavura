Zabira Technologies: Marking the Big 5.

Publish date: 2024-05-19

In today’s fintech landscape, the demand for a quick, reliable instant payment platform that also facilitates seamless digital asset transactions is paramount. This demand initially gave rise to Zabira, a platform for trading cryptocurrencies, and Grip, another platform for trading gift cards from popular brands, topping up airtime/data, funding bet wallets, and paying for cable subscriptions.

However, to enhance user experience and offer even more to our clients, we created Zabira Pro—a secure, all-in-one app that aims to address existing gaps and transform the way everyday people make payments online. Whether you’re making instant payments, exploring digital currencies, or trading gift cards, Zabira Pro provides a smooth, secure, and user-friendly experience.

As we celebrate our 5th anniversary, we not only reflect on our journey and growth from our previous platforms to this latest upgrade, but we also celebrate the users who have been instrumental in helping us build better payment solutions. We are committed to providing excellence, financial security, and an unparalleled user experience.

With the launch of Zabira Pro, we will be discontinuing our old apps, "Zabira" and "Grip," to focus on delivering the best services through Zabira Pro. To honor this transition and commemorate our anniversary, we have launched a series of reward campaigns — Zabira’s Big 5 Extravaganza — to show our appreciation to the customers who keep us in business. The campaign includes:

  • Grand Prize Giveaway of 500,000 Naira: This grand prize will be awarded through a raffle draw. Users who log in, sign up for Zabira Pro, and conduct successful transactions will be eligible. The more transactions users complete, the higher their chances of winning.
  • Five Winners Share 500,000 Naira: This includes awards for the Top Cumulative Transaction Value (awarded to the user with the highest total transaction value on Zabira Pro), participation in Zabira’s Anniversary Quiz Contest on our Telegram channel, the First Transaction on Zabira Pro, the Top Social Referrer across our social pages, and the Most Active User on Zabira Pro (awarded to the user who makes the most use of our services).
  • Top Daily Transactions: 50,000 Naira worth of USDT awarded to 10 users who sign into Zabira Pro and complete transactions above 50k from August 19th to August 23rd.
  • Airtime and Bolt Coupon Giveaways: Airtime will be given to users who engage in our Telegram channel contests, and Bolt coupons will be awarded to 100 users who sign into Zabira Pro and conduct transactions during the anniversary week.
  • UGC Content Contest: Users are encouraged to quote a promotional post on Facebook and Twitter. The posts with the most likes and retweets will win a total of 100,000 Naira on each platform.

About Zabira Technologies

Founded in August 2019, Zabira is a fintech company driven by a mission to create quick payment solutions and facilitate the easy exchange of digital assets. CEO, Isaac John launched the company with innovative ideas that have led to the development of a world-class financial technology platform.
