We Used AI to See How 15 Stars Would Look as Disney Princes and Princesses, and It Did Not Disappoin

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs was released in 1937, and it presented to the world the very first Disney princess, Snow White herself, and the first Disney prince, Prince Florian (better known as Prince Charming). Since then, many others have graced us with their adventures, and more often than not, they’re voiced by celebrities we know well, like Mandy Moore, who lent her voice to Rapunzel in Tangled (2010). So we wondered how it would be if our beloved stars went the extra mile and lent their looks to a Disney character.
