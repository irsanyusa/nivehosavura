Daniella Karagach Shares Her First Day on 'Dancing With The Stars' As a Mom

Publish date: 2024-09-02

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

ABC/Andrew Eccles

Reading Time: 2 minutes

New mom Daniella Karagach is currently preparing for the upcoming Dancing With the Stars season. On top of her ballroom prep, she’s being sure to save time for her daughter. Karagach recently gave fans a preview of her first day as a pro-slash-mom on DWTS.

Daniella Karagach Details First Day on DWTS Season 32

Karagach definitely has more responsibilities to fulfill now that she’s a mother. In her most recent Instagram upload, the DWTS pro brought her fans along as she got ready for her first day.

“All the feels @dancingwiththestars #dwts #dj #mom #dancingwiththestars,” she wrote.

The video began with Karagach cuddling her four-month-old baby Nikita beside her husband, Pasha Paskov. The video then transitioned to her showing her breast pumps, finishing her shower routine as Pashkov and Nikita barge in, breastfeeding Nikita, and fixing her hair.

Later, Karagach showed herself at the DWTS studio, then showed her celebrity partner Jason Mraz. They then held one finger up, marking their first day of rehearsals.

Upon seeing her upload, her fans were quick to send their love to her family and Mraz. Fans are obviously happy that Karagach will be back in the ballroom floor for season 32. Her supporters can’t wait to witness how Karagach and Mraz will make their professional chemistry work through dancing.

Fans are Praising Her Postpartum Figure

On September 13, Dancing With The Stars finally announced the official cast for season 32. Later, Karagach took the time to welcome Mraz via social media.

“I’M BACK BABY And I can finally share my amazing partner with you all!! @jason_mraz let’s freaking get it. You’re a rockstar and I can’t wait to watch your journey unfold,” she wrote.

However, while Karagach’s supporters expressed their warm welcome to the folk-pop singer, several others particularly pointed how the DWTS pro’s body is still the same despite giving birth.

“You look amazing and don’t even look like you had a baby,” one user wrote.

“WOOOHOOOOO!! And didn’t you have baby like 5 minutes ago?? You look amazing!” another person commented.

“Yay so happy to see you’re back Daniella! And looking gorgeous! You’re gonna rock this season with Jason!” another person wrote.

By the looks of it, Karagach is ready to win another Mirrorball trophy again. From body to mindset, the professional choreographer is absolutely in it to win it.

Dancing With The Stars will premiere on ABC and Disney+ on September 26, 2023.
