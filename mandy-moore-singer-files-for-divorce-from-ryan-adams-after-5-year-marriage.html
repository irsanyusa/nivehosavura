Singer files for divorce from Ryan Adams after 5-year marriage

Publish date: 2024-05-22

The 30-year-old singer and actress reportedly sang backup for him only few months ago for his new self-titled, album released in September last year.

The couple tied the knot back in March 2009 at Savannah, Ga., after dating for about a year.
