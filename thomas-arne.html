Thomas Arne (Composer) - On This Day

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Full Name: Thomas Augustine Arne
Profession: Composer

Biography: One of Great Britain's greatest theatrical composers of the 18th-century.

Arne is best remembered for his patriotic song "Rule, Britannia" and the popular folk song and nursery rhyme "A-Hunting We Will Go".

Born: March 12, 1710
Birthplace: Covent Garden, London, England, Great Britain
Star Sign: Pisces

Died: March 5, 1778 (aged 67)
