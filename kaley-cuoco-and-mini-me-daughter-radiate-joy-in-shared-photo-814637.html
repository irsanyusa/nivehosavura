Kaley Cuoco and Mini-Me Daughter Radiate Joy in Shared Photo

Publish date: 2024-07-15

The Big Bang Theory star Kaley Cuoco has a happy little girl on her hands! Cuoco’s baby daughter was captured with an ear-to-ear smile by actor Tom Pelphrey, dad and husband of the smiling duo. It’s impossible not to feel joy when seeing the full photo!

Recently, Ozark-star Tom Pelphrey blew the scales of cuteness by sharing a shot of his daughter Matilda ecstatic for being held by her mom, none other than actress Kaley Cuoco.

In the frame, Cuoco, 37, radiated sheer delight, mirroring the joy evident in her baby girl who was in her arms. The endearing scene showcased the little one’s strawberry-adorned bib, a charming detail that added to the heartwarming snapshot.

Cuoco and Pelphrey are each other’s biggest fans.

Cuoco and Pelphrey have only been together since April 2022. However, they never get tired of speaking highly of each other online and telling the world how much they love one another.

On July 28, 2023, the actress wished her partner happy birthday in a long text posted on Instagram where she called him “the love of [her] life” and listed other qualities of his, such as him being “best partner, best son, best friend” and above all “best daddy.”

About a month before, it was Pelphrey who opened up about Cuoco. “Love you @kaleycuoco — thanks for all the dad swag, a beautiful morning and for being the best partner in the world,” he wrote on Father’s Day special post.

Couple goals, right!?
